Rahul Bhoi

UI/UX Designer | Corporate employee | Creative Thinker

Hi, I'm Rahul Bhoi — born and raised in Parola, a small town in Jalgaon, Maharashtra. With a degree in Computer Science from North Maharashtra University and over 15 months of hands-on corporate experience, I’ve built a unique profile that blends technical expertise with creative passion.

My career began at Web Mind IT Solutions in Pune as a Technical Support Analyst. I resolved client issues, managed documentation, tested new technologies, and supported app rollouts—laying a strong foundation in IT, problem-solving, and client engagement.

Later, at Digitalza Enterprises in Navi Mumbai, I worked as a Back Office Executive & Digital Marketer. I created motion graphics, edited videos, maintained sales data, trained team members, and managed departmental budgets—fusing technical knowledge with creativity and business awareness.

I’m proficient in UI/UX Design, HTML, CSS, SQL, Excel, and tools like Jira, Salesforce, and Adobe Photoshop. I also pursue filmmaking and photography, which keeps my creative mindset sharp and improves my visual storytelling and design intuition.

Currently, I’m seeking an opportunity that challenges me both technically and creatively. If you're looking for someone who merges logic with imagination, I’d love to connect.
